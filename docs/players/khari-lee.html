Score Methodology

All-Around Score: Percentile average across all statistical categories in football leagues. Calculated by ranking each player against peers for every stat in their league-season, then averaging those percentiles. Higher scores indicate consistent excellence across multiple positions and metrics.

Position Score: Similar ranking methodology applied exclusively to statistics relevant to the player's primary position. Provides a focused measure of excellence at their specific role.
